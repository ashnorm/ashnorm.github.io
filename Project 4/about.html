WHO ARE WE

Little ghost is a small business that hosts seasonal pop-up locations in the city of Edmonton, showcasing the one-of-a-kind experiences and products we provide.
We bring focus to creating pleasant, creative atmospheres while providing relaxing environments that anyone can enjoy.

WHY

At Little Ghost, we want to give customers a space to minimize the stress from daily life and provide a relaxing, unique ambiance.
Our pop-up locations emphasize the uniqueness and exclusivity of our brand, while creating a pleasant space to stumble onto.
